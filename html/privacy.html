Privacy Agreement

This policy applies to the products and/or services provided by rockship technology co., limited, but if some products and/or services have

separate privacy clauses/privacy agreements, the separate privacy clauses/privacy agreements shall prevail, and the separate

privacy clauses/privacy agreements will be Execute first. Application. For content not mentioned, this policy applies.


1. How we collect and use your personal information

We collect personal information to provide diverse products and services, and system permissions are disabled by default, requiring

separate consent. Information is obtained through data you actively provide and data collected automatically, which is used for

essential functions such as registration, login, and data maintenance.


2. Service content browsing, searching, and playing

When you browse, search, or play content, we collect device information and usage records to ensure a smooth and consistent experience.

Search-related data may be temporarily stored on your device for faster display, while clipboard content is only displayed locally and

uploaded to the server if you confirm a search.


3. Information production and release

When using posting and commenting features, you actively provide text, images, audio, or video that we collect and

display together with your nickname and avatar. You must obtain legal authorization before posting others’ personal information,

and these functions require permission to access your camera, photo album, and microphone.


4. Service content display and recommendation

We collect your online activity records and basic information to analyze preferences and improve content recommendations using

de-identified data and machine-learning technology. Our recommendation system cannot identify the true identity of any specific

natural person.


5. Other functions

For features not listed or when collecting information beyond stated purposes, we will notify you again and obtain your

consent in accordance with the law. Personal information may be processed without consent when required for legal obligations,

contract performance, public health emergencies, or lawful public interests.


6. Storage of personal information

We retain personal information only for the minimum period necessary to provide services, such as keeping your mobile number

while your account is active. Once data is no longer needed, deleted by the user, or exceeds the retention period,

it will be deleted or anonymized.


7. Protection of minors

We take minors’ personal information seriously, and the definition of a minor depends on applicable laws in different regions.

Minors should read this policy with a guardian and provide personal information only after obtaining proper consent.


8. Updates to this policy

This policy may be updated in response to product changes or legal requirements, and we encourage users to review

it regularly. Any updates will be communicated through internal notifications or other appropriate methods,

and your personal rights will not be infringed without explicit consent.
