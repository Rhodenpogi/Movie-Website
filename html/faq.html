Frequently Asked Questions

1. How to subscribe?

Users can subscribe by tapping the subscription banner on the home page or by selecting any premium movie. Subscription gives

access to unlimited, ad-free premium movies. If subscription fails, check whether your payment method is supported.


2. How to adjust the movie quality?

Video quality depends on the main or third-party server streaming the movie. Users can try adjusting quality in the video settings.

If no quality option appears, switch to another server or check the internet connection.


3. Why can’t the movie be played?

Playback issues happen because the site redirects to external servers. If a movie doesn’t load, shows errors, or excessive ads,

try switching servers, reloading the site, checking internet connection, verifying subscription (for premium movies),

or reporting it to the admins.


4. Why did my valid subscription disappear?

The system removes subscriptions only when they expire. If it disappears before expiration, it may be a system error.

Reload the website, check your subscription status, ensure stable internet, or contact the admins if the issue continues.


5. Why is the website laggy or freezing?

Lag can be caused by heavy streaming traffic, excessive ads, or system maintenance. Solutions include reloading the site,

subscribing to remove ads, checking your internet, clearing cache/RAM, or contacting admins if the issue persists.
